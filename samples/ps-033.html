Delivery

A vaccine delivers instructions to your cells to create a unique spike protein. This protein is associated with COVID-19 but is harmless to you in this form. Once these instructions are delivered, the delivery method is eliminated from your body.

Teaching Your Immune System

Your immune system senses the foreign protein and kills it off. Through this process, your body learns how to detect SARS-CoV-2 and how to fight it.
